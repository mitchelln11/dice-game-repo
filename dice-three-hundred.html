Dice Cricket

The goal of this game is to "close out" the numbers 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, and bullseye. You can accomplish this by hitting these numbers three times.

  • Close out the numbers 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, and bullseye(25).
  • If one player has already closed out a number, they will get points in the amount they roll, as long as the opposing player has NOT closed out the same number.
  • The first player to close out all numbers AND have the highest score wins.
  • If a value other than 15, 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, or bullseye(25) is rolled, no points will be given, and no numbers will close out. They are considered misses.

  • Shoot for Bullseye - Possibilities Bullseye(25),1,4,7,8
  • Shoot for 20 - Possibilities 20,1,5,18,12
  • Shoot for 18 - Possibilities 18,4,1,20,13,6,5
  • Shoot for 16 - Possibilities 16,8,11,7,19,3
  • Shoot for 15 - Possibilities 15,10,2,6,17
  • Shoot for 19 or 17 - Possibilities 19,3,7,17,Bullseye(25)
